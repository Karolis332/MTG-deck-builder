# The Cabbage Merchant — Ultimate Historic Brawl Deck (v2)

> *"My cabbages..."*

## Strategy

Food token engine. Opponents cast noncreature spells -> you get Food. Combat damage -> sacrifice Food (defensive). Tap two Foods -> any color mana (ramp). The deck snowballs Food generation into card advantage, board presence, and overwhelming value.

**Key synergy lines:**
- Academy Manufactor triples every Food into Food + Clue + Treasure
- Jaheira, Friend of the Forest makes ALL tokens tap for {G} (including Clues + Treasures from Manufactor)
- Night of the Sweets' Revenge makes each Food tap for {G} AND gives creatures +2/+2
- Trail of Crumbs draws cards every time you sacrifice Food
- Peregrin Took doubles Food generation and converts surplus to cards
- Displaced Dinosaurs turns every Food token into a 7/7
- Stridehangar Automaton adds a 1/1 flying Thopter with every artifact token batch
- Nykthos, Shrine to Nyx generates 5-8+ mana with green devotion from all our permanents

## Dream Scenarios

1. **T1** Llanowar Elves -> **T2** The Cabbage Merchant -> **T3** Academy Manufactor. Every opponent noncreature spell now gives you Food + Clue + Treasure.

2. **Jaheira + Manufactor + Commander**: Opponent casts a spell -> you get Food + Clue + Treasure -> all three tap for {G} -> 3 free mana per opponent spell.

3. **Displaced Dinosaurs + Manufactor**: Every Food trigger creates three 7/7 creatures. One opponent spell = three 7/7s.

4. **Stridehangar Automaton + Manufactor**: Every Food trigger creates Food + Clue + Treasure + Thopter. Four tokens per trigger, and the Thopter flies.

5. **Night of the Sweets' Revenge + 10 Foods**: All creatures get +2/+2, 10 Foods tap for 10 green mana. Cast your entire hand.

## Matchup Notes

- **vs. Control/Spellslinger**: Commander generates massive Food value. They cast spells, you ramp. Jaheira converts all tokens to mana.
- **vs. Aggro**: Food sacrifice absorbs combat damage. Wicked Wolf removes key threats. Night of the Sweets' Revenge pumps blockers.
- **vs. Midrange**: Out-value them with Academy Manufactor + Trail of Crumbs engine. Stridehangar adds flying Thopters for evasion.
- **vs. Combo**: Heroic Intervention + Beast Within + Nature's Claim are your interaction. Race with Displaced Dinosaurs 7/7 army.
